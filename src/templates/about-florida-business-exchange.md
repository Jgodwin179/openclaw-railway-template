# Florida Business Exchange (FBX)

## Overview

Florida Business Exchange (FBX) is a business brokerage and mergers & acquisitions advisory firm that helps business owners sell their companies and helps qualified buyers acquire businesses.

The firm specializes in lower middle market transactions and operates primarily in Florida but works with buyers and investors nationwide.

FBX represents business owners in confidential sale processes designed to maximize value by creating competitive buyer interest.

---

# Mission

The mission of Florida Business Exchange is to help business owners achieve successful exits by connecting them with qualified buyers and running a structured sales process that maximizes value.

Most business owners sell their company only once in their lifetime. FBX exists to guide them through that process professionally.

---

# What FBX Does

Florida Business Exchange primarily provides **sell-side M&A advisory services**.

These services include:

- Business valuations
- Exit planning
- Preparing businesses for sale

- Managing buyer outreach
- Negotiating deal terms
- Managing due diligence
- Coordinating with attorneys, CPAs, and lenders
- Helping transactions close successfully

FBX acts as the **intermediary between the seller and the buyer** throughout the process.

# How the Sale Process Works

The typical business sale process involves several key phases.

### 1. Discovery

The first step is understanding the business.

This includes reviewing:

- financial statements
- tax returns
- operations
- market position
- ownership goals

FBX determines whether the business is ready to go to market.

---

### 2. Valuation

FBX performs a valuation to determine the likely market value of the business.

Valuations consider:

- revenue
- seller's discretionary earnings (SDE)
- EBITDA
- industry multiples
- growth trends
- customer concentration
- risk factors

This helps establish a realistic asking price.

---

### 3. Preparing the Business for Market

FBX prepares marketing materials including:

- Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)
- financial summaries
- operational summaries
- buyer qualification criteria

Confidentiality is extremely important during this phase.

---

### 4. Buyer Outreach

The business is marketed confidentially to qualified buyers.

Interested buyers must:

1. sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
2. provide financial qualifications
3. demonstrate acquisition capability

Once approved, they receive detailed information about the business.

---

### 5. Offers and Negotiation

Buyers submit offers typically in the form of:

Letter of Intent (LOI)

FBX helps the seller evaluate offers based on:

- price
- structure
- financing terms
- buyer capability
- closing timeline

The goal is to select the best overall buyer.

---

### 6. Due Diligence

After the LOI is accepted, the buyer performs due diligence.

This includes reviewing:

- financial records
- contracts
- customer data
- operations
- legal documents

FBX helps coordinate document flow and keep the deal moving.

---

### 7. Closing

Attorneys prepare the final purchase agreement and closing documents.

Once all conditions are satisfied, the transaction closes and ownership transfers to the buyer.

---

# Why Business Owners Use FBX

Many buyers attempt to contact business owners directly to buy their company.

These buyers often want to avoid competition so they can acquire the business at a lower price.

FBX helps create a competitive process so multiple buyers can evaluate the opportunity.

Competition between buyers typically increases:

- purchase price
- deal quality
- likelihood of closing

The goal is to ensure the business owner receives **full market value** for the company.

# Core Principles

Florida Business Exchange operates on several core principles.

### Confidentiality

Business sales must remain confidential to protect employees, customers, and vendor relationships.

---

### Qualified Buyers

Not every interested party is a serious buyer.

FBX screens buyers to ensure they have:

- financial capability
- acquisition experience
- real intent

---

### Competitive Process

The best way to maximize value is to create buyer competition.

FBX structures marketing processes designed to attract multiple buyers.

---

### Professional Deal Management

Most transactions take **6-12 months** from start to close.

FBX manages the process to avoid delays and reduce deal risk.
