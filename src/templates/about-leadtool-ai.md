# Leadtool.ai

## Overview

Leadtool.ai is a RevOps and sales infrastructure platform that helps B2B companies build and manage automated sales and marketing systems.

The platform focuses on building technical infrastructure that supports outbound sales, lead management, and customer acquisition.

Leadtool.ai provides the systems that power modern outbound sales teams.

---

# Mission

The mission of Leadtool.ai is to help companies deploy complete sales and marketing systems without needing large internal technical teams.

---

# What Leadtool.ai Does

Leadtool.ai builds and manages the **technical infrastructure behind modern sales operations**.

This includes:

- CRM environments
- automation workflows
- marketing funnels
- lead tracking systems
- appointment booking systems
- sales pipelines

---
